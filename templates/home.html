HOW DOES THE SUMMARIZER WORK?

You can automatically generate a summary of any article you want.
First copy paste the article/text you want a summary of (You can write the article in the textbox or copy from another resource).
Then paste it in the text box and click on the Summarize button.

Text Summarizer that uses NLP (Natural Language Processing) to generate a summary of the given article or text. The Text Summarizer uses an Extractive Summarization method which attempts to summarize articles by selecting a subset of words that retain the most important points. Weights are assigned to the important part of sentences using this approach which is then used to form the summary.

RULES OF SUMMARIZING

Whether you are summarizing manually or using a summarization tool, you should follow the rules listed below:

1. When summarizing, make sure you are covering all the points in the actual context to maintain the concept of the writer.
2. Your summary should be concise including the informative concepts only. Do not include the unessential passages in your summary.
3.The summary can represent the author’s idea. The original idea shouldn’t be inaccurate.
4.Don’t repeat any points more than once. You should also not add your points.
5. Combine the ideas of the original content while it should be matching with the other sentences.
6. In summary, you should use the words of the category instead of the object.
